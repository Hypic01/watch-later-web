The method

Three steps, about five minutes

Nothing here touches your YouTube account. The reading happens in your own browser; the filing happens here.

01

Add the extension

YouTube blocks apps from reading Watch Later, so the reading happens in your own browser. Install it, sign in, and the whole list syncs in one click. Any browser without it can paste the list in by hand.

02

The librarian reads

Every video is filed on one of five shelves, each with a one line reason in plain language. Re-file anything and it learns your taste for next time.

03

Clear the shelves

Mark things done, dismiss the dead weight, and take the checklist back to YouTube. From then on new saves are filed automatically, so it never rots again.
